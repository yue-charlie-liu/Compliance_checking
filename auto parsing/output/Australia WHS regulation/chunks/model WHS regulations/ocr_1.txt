Model Work Health and Safety Regulations 

as at 5 December 2025 

As released by Safe Work Australia Published by the Parliamentary Counsel’s Committee 

This is a consolidated version of the Model Work Health and Safety Regulations. These Regulations are a national model law and are intended to provide the basis for nationally consistent work health and safety laws. These Regulations do not, by themselves, have any legal effect.
